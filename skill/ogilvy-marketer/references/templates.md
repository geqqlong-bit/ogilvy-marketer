# Templates

Use these template IDs when building the workflow payload.

## `launch_cn`
Use for new-product launches, first-wave market entry, message house design, and phased launch planning.

## `promotion_cn`
Use for shopping festivals, seasonal pushes, conversion campaigns, and budget allocation for a promotion window.

## `content_matrix_cn`
Use for always-on content planning, seed-and-grow Xiaohongshu or Douyin work, topic libraries, and angle generation.

## `weekly_report_cn`
Use for performance review, weekly reporting, attribution discussion, and follow-up optimization recommendations.

## Selection heuristic

- New product, GTM, first campaign -> `launch_cn`
- Big sales node, promo push, ROI pressure -> `promotion_cn`
- Competitor scanning, content ideas, social planning -> `content_matrix_cn`
- Review, recap, optimization memo -> `weekly_report_cn`
